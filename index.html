What you will learn

AI chatbots for ecommerce are software applications that use artificial intelligence to simulate conversations with customers. They can help with tasks such as answering frequently asked questions, providing product recommendations, and processing orders.

By automating these tasks, ecommerce chatbots can improve customer satisfaction, reduce support queries, and increase sales.
